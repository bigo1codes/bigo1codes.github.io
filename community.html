Big-O 1 Community

Big-O 1 runs free WhatsApp support groups for many modules offered in many different institutions. Students join and interact with Big-O 1 programming tutors and other students.

All groups are closely monitored by our tutors so that the content is strictly about that particular module. We will gladly support anyone who is doing any kind of programming, and we mean ANY. So if you don't find a group for your module here contact us so we can create one and assign you a tutor.
